# Aurora

DJ-style frequency-coloured waveforms for your DAW. A VST3/AU plugin for Windows and macOS, built with JUCE.

![Aurora main interface](https://i0.wp.com/schematicsound.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Aurora_expanded_cropped.png?w=540&ssl=1)
![Aurora compact interface](https://i0.wp.com/schematicsound.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Aurora_compact_cropped.png?w=540&ssl=1)

## What is it?

Aurora brings DJ-style coloured waveforms (think Rekordbox, Traktor, Serato) directly into your DAW. View the frequency balance of your mix and how instruments interact, in real time, without exporting and re-importing clips.

Aurora isn't a 1:1 clone of any DJ software — it aims for a consistent, controllable relationship between colour and frequency balance.

## Features

- **Compact mode**: hide controls and shrink the window to a thin meter strip when you just need a glance.
- **Adjustable crossovers**: tune Low and High crossover points to isolate sub-bass, vocals, cymbals, etc.
- **Colour mixer**: fine-tune how each frequency band contributes to the final waveform colour.
- **Adjustable scroll speed**: zoom in for detail or slow down for the big picture.
- **Channel Selection:** Mono (L+R) and single-channel (L/R) modes.
- **Themes:** Light/Dark and more customizability coming soon.
- **Pause waveform scrolling** during playback by clicking the waveform.

## Download

As of **v1.1.0**, Aurora is open source. The licensing and activation system has been removed, and the plugin is now pay-what-you-want — support development if you find it useful.

Installers (Windows 10/11 64-bit VST3, macOS 13+ Intel/Apple Silicon VST3/AU) are available at [https://schematicsound.com/aurora](https://schematicsound.com/aurora).
